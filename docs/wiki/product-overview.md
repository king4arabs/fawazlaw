# Product Overview

Fawazlaw.sa is the digital home and growth engine of **شركة فواز للمحاماة والاستشارات القانونية**, a Saudi law firm serving individuals, SMEs, and enterprises across Saudi Arabia and the wider GCC.

## What the Product Does Today
- Presents the firm's practice areas, partners, and contact pathways in Arabic-first, mobile-first surfaces.
- Provides SEO-optimised landing pages for the top legal services.
- Provides legal articles and insights.
- Provides a contact funnel (form, WhatsApp, phone) that routes to firm intake.
- Provides an admin layer (Laravel + Node) to manage services, articles, contacts, and payment-linked content.
- Positions an AI legal assistant clearly as a support tool, not legal advice.

## What the Product Will Do
- Authenticated client portal with encrypted document workflows.
- Booking and payments end-to-end via MyFatoorah (mada / Apple Pay / STC Pay).
- AI-assisted intake + summarisation with mandatory human review.
- PDPL data-subject request workflow (access / correct / delete / export).
- Bilingual (Arabic / English) parity on the top service surfaces.
- GCC content variants.

## Target Outcomes
- Increase qualified consultation volume month over month.
- Establish topical authority in Saudi legal search.
- Reduce administrative overhead for the practice via the client portal.
- Provide a PDPL-defensible, audit-ready stack for enterprise procurement.
