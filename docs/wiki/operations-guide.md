## Cadences
- **Daily** — uptime, error tracker, support triage, smoke test of CTAs.
- **Weekly** — dependency advisories, analytics, Lighthouse, backlog grooming.
- **Monthly** — backup-restore drill, access review, benchmark update.
- **Quarterly** — pricing/strategy review, tabletop incident exercise.
