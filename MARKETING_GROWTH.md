# Marketing & Growth

## SEO strategy
Build Arabic-first legal service clusters, English support pages, technical SEO, schema markup, page speed, internal links, and topical authority.

## Content strategy
Publish practice area guides, FAQs, legal process explainers, Saudi regulation updates, case-study-style trust content, and service landing pages.

## Arabic/English growth strategy
Arabic is primary for Saudi trust and search volume. English pages support expatriates, investors, and enterprise clients.

## Legal service landing pages
Create clear pages by service: consultation, contracts, corporate, disputes, employment, real estate, compliance, and litigation support.

## Conversion optimization
Use clear calls-to-action, trust badges, service benefits, contact options, pricing where appropriate, fast forms, and reassurance copy.

## Lead generation
Track consultation requests, contact forms, service inquiries, phone/email clicks, newsletter signups, and content downloads.

## Analytics setup
Use privacy-aware analytics with conversion events, source tracking, funnels, and dashboards.

## Social proof
Add testimonials, credentials, media mentions, professional memberships, and anonymized results where ethically permitted.

## Authority-building content
Publish reviewed legal guides, checklists, explainers, and updates for Saudi/GCC business and individuals.

## Saudi legal market positioning
Position Fawazlaw.sa as professional, confidential, Arabic-first, digitally accessible, and business-ready.
