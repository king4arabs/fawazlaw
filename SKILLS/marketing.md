# Marketing Playbook

## Purpose
Drive qualified, Saudi-first organic and paid demand to Fawazlaw.sa, build legal authority, and convert visitors into consultations — without overstating the firm's commitments.

## Standards
- Every public page has a unique title, meta description, canonical URL, and OG/Twitter card.
- Every public claim is verifiable.
- Every conversion event is named and instrumented.
- Arabic is the primary content language; English ships only when human-translated and reviewed.

## Checklist
- [ ] Page meets the landing-page structure in `MARKETING_GROWTH.md`.
- [ ] Structured data validated with Google Rich Results Test.
- [ ] Internal links to ≥ 3 related articles/services.
- [ ] Mobile sticky CTA renders correctly.
- [ ] Conversion events fire and are visible in analytics within 1 hour.

## Best Practices
- Build topic clusters: a hub service page + 5–10 supporting articles.
- Refresh the top 20 pages quarterly; mark "last reviewed".
- Use real testimonials with consent; never fabricate.
- Run small, hypothesis-driven experiments and record results in `DECISIONS.md`.
- Watch Search Console for impressions/CTR drift on key Arabic queries.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Stuffing keywords; modern search penalises it and clients distrust it.
- Promising legal outcomes.
- Publishing English machine translations of Arabic legal content.
- Adding more CTAs to a page instead of clarifying the primary one.

## Project-Specific Implementation Guidance
- Local SEO: prioritise Riyadh and Jeddah first; add city pages only when there is verified service presence.
- Practice-area landing pages must avoid jargon in the H1 and intro; keep depth in the FAQ and supporting articles.
- Lead capture forms have at most 3 required fields on first contact.

## Recommended Tools
- Google Search Console, Google Analytics 4 (or Plausible), Ahrefs/Semrush, Schema Markup Validator, Lighthouse.

## Validation Steps
- Verify metadata + structured data on every new public page.
- Verify conversion events fire.
- Monthly review of top 20 queries and pages.

## Definition of Done
- Page indexed, structured data valid, internal links in place, analytics confirmed.
- `MARKETING_GROWTH.md` updated if strategy shifted.
