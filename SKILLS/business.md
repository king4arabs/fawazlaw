# Business Playbook

## Purpose
Translate Fawazlaw.sa's legal-practice strengths into sustainable, ethically marketed revenue across consultations, retainers, productised packages, and (later) subscription portal access.

## Standards
- All marketing claims are verifiable and Bar-compliant.
- Pricing pages name what is included and what is not.
- Service packaging is reviewed by a practitioner.
- Customer journey from search → contact → consultation → engagement is instrumented.

## Checklist
- [ ] Service has a written scope, deliverables, exclusions, and pricing model.
- [ ] Pricing reviewed against market and firm profitability.
- [ ] Practitioner ownership assigned.
- [ ] CRM record opens automatically on first contact.

## Best Practices
- Productise repeatable matters (incorporation, employment kit, contract review).
- Offer a clear consultation product as the universal entry point.
- Reserve retainers for clients with predictable monthly legal work.
- Use case-study marketing (anonymised) to build authority.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Underpricing to win volume — legal work has a quality floor that volume erodes.
- Vague service descriptions ("legal support") that leak scope.
- Mixing marketing claims with regulatory promises ("guaranteed outcomes" — never).
- Letting paid ads outrun the firm's capacity to serve quality.

## Project-Specific Implementation Guidance
- Pricing for SAR is the default; show VAT-inclusive prices.
- Display payment methods accepted (mada, Apple Pay, STC Pay, cards) clearly when MyFatoorah is enabled.
- Enterprise contact pathway separate from individual consultation (different fields, different SLA).

## Recommended Tools
- HubSpot / Pipedrive / Zoho CRM (Saudi-region data residency where available).
- Stripe / MyFatoorah dashboards for revenue.
- Notion / Coda for service catalog.

## Validation Steps
- Quarterly pricing review with managing partners.
- Monthly funnel review (search → contact → consult → engagement).
- Annual win/loss analysis.

## Definition of Done
- Service listed in the public catalog with full scope and price.
- Practitioner owner named.
- CRM stage map updated.
- Recorded in `BUSINESS_CONTEXT.md` if it changes positioning.
